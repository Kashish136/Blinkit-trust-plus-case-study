# 🚀 Project Overview

Quick-commerce has transformed convenience, but pricing transparency remains one of the biggest trust challenges.

Users frequently discover additional charges only at checkout, leading to confusion, abandoned carts, and reduced trust.

This case study investigates how Blinkit can improve transparency while maintaining revenue growth and customer satisfaction.

The result is **Blinkit Trust+**, a product ecosystem designed to make pricing predictable, understandable, and trustworthy.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

**How might Blinkit increase revenue through transparent pricing while maintaining user trust and improving the overall customer experience?**

Rather than eliminating fees, this project focuses on helping users understand **why** they are paying them.

---

# 💡 Key Insights

The strongest insight discovered during research was:

> **Users don't mind paying extra—they mind not knowing why they're paying.**

Customers are willing to pay convenience fees when:

- Pricing is transparent
- Charges are explained
- Expectations are set early
- There are no surprises during checkout

Trust matters more than discounts.
